Per-approach performance on CIFAR-100

Grouped by training / robustness approach · Bars show Standard Accuracy, Certified Robust Rate (CRR), and Certified Robust Accuracy (CRA).

How to read this chart

  • The x-axis shows the training / defence approach (ERM, DA, PGDT, TRADES, MART, RS, IBP, PRL, TandT).
  • The y-axis is the percentage value (0–100%) for the selected dataset.
  • For each approach, three blue bars appear: Std (standard accuracy), CRR (certified robust rate), CRA (certified robust accuracy).
  • Use the buttons above the chart to switch between CIFAR-100, CIFAR-10, SVHN, and MNIST without losing any data.
  • Bars animate in from zero on page load (non-interaction transition), and smoothly update when you change datasets.
